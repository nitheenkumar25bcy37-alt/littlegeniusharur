Curriculum & Learning

A Curriculum That Grows With Your Child

Structured programmes from playgroup to primary, designed to spark curiosity and build solid academic foundations.

🍼

Playgroup (1.5–2.5 yrs)

Sensory exploration, music, movement and social play in a warm, nurturing setting.

🎨

Nursery (2.5–3.5 yrs)

Language development, art, storytelling and structured play activities that build early literacy.

🔤

LKG (3.5–4.5 yrs)

Phonics, numbers, Tamil & English writing, logical thinking through interactive lessons.

📖

UKG (4.5–5.5 yrs)

Advanced reading, writing, mathematical concepts, and personality development activities.

🌍

Bilingual Learning

Tamil and English instruction with age-appropriate balance ensuring natural multilingual development.

🎵

Arts & Music

Weekly music, dance, clay-work and visual arts cultivate creativity and emotional expression.
